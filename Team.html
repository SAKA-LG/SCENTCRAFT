Organizational Structure

At ScentCraft

At ScentCraft, we embrace a horizontal (flat) structure because we value flexibility, creativity, and rapid decision-making — all of which are essential in the fast-paced, ever-evolving fragrance industry.

  • Faster Decision-Making: With fewer levels of management, our teams — from scent design to marketing — can directly communicate with leadership. This accelerates approvals, shortens innovation cycles, and lets us respond quickly to trends.
  • Enhanced Creativity & Collaboration: Our flat structure empowers team members to share ideas across departments. Whether you’re in branding or bottle design, your voice matters. No bureaucracy, just pure creative flow.
  • Empowering Every Voice: From junior perfumers to marketing associates, everyone has ownership and impact. This sense of trust drives motivation and results in passionate, purpose-driven work.
  • Agility in a Trend-Driven Market: Fragrance trends shift fast. Our structure helps us pivot marketing strategies, product offerings, and packaging styles — efficiently and effectively.
  • Authentic Brand Identity: A flat organization helps our team stay close to the customer experience and brand mission — creating genuine, human-centered storytelling in every scent we deliver.

Team Structure & Job Descriptions

  • CEO – Chief Executive Officer: Leads the company’s overall vision and strategy. Responsible for major decisions, team building, financial oversight, and aligning every department with the company’s mission of empowering self-expression through scent.
  • COO – Chief Operating Officer: Translates vision into action. Oversees daily operations, production, logistics, supply chain, and quality control. Ensures everything runs smoothly, scalably, and sustainably.
  • CTO – Chief Technology Officer: Drives innovation and product development. Manages tech infrastructure and digital platforms that allow users to design custom fragrances. Leads R&D and keeps us on the cutting edge of perfume tech.
  • CIO – Chief Information Officer: Manages all IT systems, digital security, and infrastructure. Ensures that our technology investments align with business needs and improve operational efficiency.
  • CFO – Chief Financial Officer: Oversees all financial planning, budgeting, cash flow, and investment decisions. Plays a key role in achieving profitability and financial sustainability.
  • CMO – Chief Marketing Officer: Leads brand development and growth strategy. Manages digital marketing, brand voice, partnerships, and customer experience across all platforms — traditional and digital.
  • CEO: Leads the brand, vision, and high-level decisions
  • COO: Handles day-to-day operations & production flow
  • CTO: Manages product development, including perfume formulation tech
  • (…and so on for other roles)
